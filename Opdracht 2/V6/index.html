I believe in creating stylish and memorable visual identities that help my clients express themselves.

FKA twigs

This is a school assignment for Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (HvA). The assignment was to create a full visual identity for a musical artist. In my case the artist is FKA twigs, and I designed a visual identity for her potential new album. The concept is based on the dance style called Vogue which FKA twigs often uses in her videoclips, performances and fotoshoots. This is a dance style that involves elegant and fluent hand movements. I used outlines of FKA twigsâ€™ own hands, traced from photos of her. Inside of these outlines I have used photomanipulation to implement my own photographs of stylized black and white hands.

Awoke

Awoke is both a traditional and a web based magazine which purpose is to promote an alternative view on pop culture. The magazine expresses interesting opinions on lifestyle, fashion, music and art. The branding creates a mix of classical and modern elements to form a contemporary yet stylish identity.

Crudo

Crudo is a hip, fresh and new raw food restaurant located in Nijmegen, The Netherlands. I designed their corporate branding, logo, website and print while working for 'Byron Reclamebureau'. The overal objective of the design is to be appetizing and represent the freshness of the products from the restaurant. The webdesign is designed around a 'one-page' principle.

About
